Beagle

2 MONTH

Behavior

To this age beagles spend most of the time sleeping. But while awake, expect them to be playful and mischievous with their siblings and mother.

5 MONTH

Behavior

To this age beagles will be running around all over the house, chewing on things, and even biting you.

9 MONTH

Behavior

will still try to test their boundaries now and then. You will often find them chewing on furniture or notice other destructive behaviors.
